Writing & Creative

In my writing practice, I’m interested in exploring another way language passes through the body. I’m curious about words of worlds that speak to emotional clarity and situational opacity, relational threads weaving and unweaving. I also am a dilettante of scent art, and love it for its inherent properties of subjectivity and its elemental and time-based nature.
